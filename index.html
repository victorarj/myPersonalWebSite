About Me

Salesforce Developer

Hello! I’m Victor Araujo, a Salesforce Developer with a comprehensive background spanning various industries such as finance, healthcare, telecommunications, and retail. Over the years, I have gained hands-on experience in solution architecture, CRM integrations, and the development of customized Salesforce applications and automations that address complex business needs. Currently, my goal is to transition into a Technical Project Manager role, where I can merge my technical expertise with my interest in project leadership. Through my work in consulting firms like Wipro, Cognizant, and Deloitte, I’ve developed strong skills in aligning cross-functional teams, understanding and documenting business requirements, and delivering impactful solutions. I’m also a strong advocate for user-centered design and efficiency in process management, having led the development of custom components, complex integrations, and personalized user experiences across diverse projects. With a passion for continuous learning, I hold certifications in Platform Developer I and II, JavaScript Developer, and an MBA in Solution Architecture. My commitment to professional growth and project management excellence keeps me motivated to create high-impact solutions that meet today’s evolving business challenges. I look forward to connecting and exploring ways we can innovate together.
